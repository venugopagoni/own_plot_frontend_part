How It Works
From land to legal ownership in 5 steps
01

Create Your Account

Sign up as an owner, buyer, developer, or premium subscriber. KYC verification included.

02

Search or List a Plot

Buyers search by location, survey number, or budget. Owners list with a map pin and documents.

03

Verify Ownership

Admin reviews uploaded documents and links plot to survey number. Green badge issued.

04

Connect Directly

Buyer contacts owner via secure in-app chat. No broker, no commission, just transparent communication.

05

Transfer Ownership

New owner uploads registration proof. Admin verifies and updates platform ownership record.
